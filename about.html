About Superlative
Coffee services.

Its all About Coffee.

Founded in 2022 and fully registered as per The companies Act No1 of 2012, Superlative coffee services Ltd aims at becoming the leading service providing company in bridging the gap between international coffee buyers and local coffee suppliers, green coffee sourcing, acting as negotiators between the buyers and sellers, coffee quality control, pre-loading & loading inspection, export documentation, logistics specialists, container inspection, barista training, roasting training, cupping and quality lab; the heart of our business, specialty brewing, coffee warehouse managers and agronomy services strictly following the guidelines of Uganda Coffee Development Authority (UCDA).
The competence of the directors who have vast experience of more than 10 years in the coffee business and the relationship they have with almost all the coffee processors and exporters countrywide enables them to receive the best offers/prices therefore assuring our potential clients of the best service.

Company Profile

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between buyers and exporters while offering the world the quality coffee at the best price as well as offering top notch services to our clients.

Vision

To provide high quality coffee while socioeconomically impacting the community.

Objective

At Superlative coffee services limited quality service delivery is one of our cornerstones; to that we have built a dedicated team of green coffee quality experts, R and Q graders. Coffee quality is the basis for our activities. We control this quality systematically IO mdke sure that our buyers/clients receive the best quality coffee and competitive offers/prices.
