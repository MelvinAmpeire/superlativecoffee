Services

Company: Superlative Coffee Services (u) Ltd.

Green coffee sourcing

Green coffee sourcing from coffee farmers,processors and exporters. With the great and continuous relationship we have with with farmers,processors and exporters this gives us assurance that we can not fail to get any quantity and good quality green coffee which our clients would require, any grade of coffee.(Robusta and Arabica)

Coffee Brokerage

Acting as negotiators between the buyer and the seller coming up with prices that can be favourable on both sides.

Quality Coffee Produce

Ensuring that our Clients Recieve good Quality Coffee through guiding the sellers on how to attain the required standard by buyers.

PRE-LOADING INSPECTION.

Taking pre-loading samples on readily available lots/stacks for analysis before coffee is being loaded for quality assurance.

Quality assurance

Ensure quality is of high standard with our well equipped laboratory and expert green coffee graders and senior coffee cuppers with both Q and R grading certificates (Our annalysis is properly done and quality report issued with both defective bean analysis and cup tasting results).

Logistics specialists

Logistics specialists coordinating with clearing agents and shipping lines on timely allocation of empty containers to avoid delays on loading as it's a key for exporters in tum over their cash flow and fulfilling other contracts they may be having

Container inspection

Container inspection to ensure that the container positioned by the clearing/Forwarding agents has no holes, odour free. Proper/well alined with proper and clean liner papers or bulk liners, so that there’s no interference with the Quality of coffee.

-LOADING INSPECTION

Making sure that our representative is available at the loading site at the time of loading to make sure that quality remains the same by taking samples on each and every bag which enters the container and as well to ensure that no damaged,stained bag which enters the container. with this we avoid claims which may arise upon arrival at the final destination.

Handling export documents

Handling export documents in the most efficient and professional Way to avoid amendments which may incur time loss, as well cause demurrage Charges if the vessel arrives destination without documents.

Coffee consultancy

-how to start exporting coffee
-how to start importing coffee
-how to maintain good quality coffee for export
-starting roasted coffee business -Consultation on everything related to coffee business(roasted/Green)

agronomy

Training coffee warehouse managers to ensure proper storage of the coffee.
